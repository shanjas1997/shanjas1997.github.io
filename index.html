I'm the One

My name is Jason Shan, a third-year computer science major at the University of California, Berkeley . My focus is on theory, particularly approximation algorithms, game theory, control theory, auction theory, and machine learning.

Currently, I am a Technical Product Management Intern in the DTLP program at General Electric . My assignment is in implementing a new financial modelling software designed to replace the current one. At University, I am an undergraduate researcher currently working on the Berkeley Bell Labs team participating in the second iteration of the DARPA Spectrum Collaboration Challenge under the supervision of professors Anant Sahai, Jean Walrand and John Wawrzynek, working on algorithmic game theory.

In my free time, I like to write and play music, both instrumental and vocal, trade in the stock market, work out, and learn through reading publications finance and the theory fields.

I am constantly looking for opportunities especially in algorithmic game theory. If you have desires to keep connected or talk about a possible opportunity, feel free to email me.
